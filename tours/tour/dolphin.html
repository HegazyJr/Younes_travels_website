Highlight
  • Swim with wild dolphins in the world-renowned Dolphin House area — a rare and magical experience in their natural habitat.
  • Two snorkeling stops at vibrant coral reef sites full of marine life and colorful fish.
  • Delicious breakfast and buffet lunch served fresh on board with local and international dishes.
  • Cold drinks and fresh seasonal fruits available throughout the trip to keep you refreshed.
  • Free hotel transfers from Hurghada, El Gouna, Makadi Bay, and Sahl Hasheesh.
  • Professional multilingual guide (Arabic, English, German, Russian) ensuring safety and insight.
Destinations
  • Hurghada > Coral Reef 1 > Dolphin House > Coral Reef 2
Description
  • Begin your day with a unique sea adventure from your hotel doorstep to the crystal-clear waters of the Red Sea.
  • The boat departs from Hurghada Marina, heading towards Dolphin House — a top destination for spotting and swimming with wild dolphins in their natural habitat.
  • The trip continues with two snorkeling sessions at different reef locations, where you’ll dive into colorful coral gardens and witness an array of tropical fish.
  • It’s a rare opportunity to connect with nature while floating peacefully in warm, turquoise waters.
  • Onboard, you’ll enjoy a light and tasty breakfast, followed by a fresh buffet lunch with a selection of local and international dishes.
  • Cold drinks and seasonal fruits are provided all day to keep you refreshed.
  • Designed for both excitement and comfort, the trip follows a well-structured itinerary that ensures you make the most of every moment — whether you're snorkeling, relaxing, or just enjoying the stunning views.
  • The journey ends with your return to the marina and comfortable drop-off at your hotel, filled with memories of dolphins, reef adventures, and pure Red Sea bliss.
