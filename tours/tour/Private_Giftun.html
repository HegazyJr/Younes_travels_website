description
  • Enjoy a luxurious day at sea aboard a private boat heading toward Giftun Island—a hidden gem in Egypt's Red Sea,
  • known for its calm turquoise waters and white sandy beaches.
  • Your day begins with hotel pickup in Hurghada, El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, or Makadi.
  • Upon arrival at the marina, you'll board your private boat and sail in comfort and privacy.
  • Start your adventure with two consecutive snorkeling sessions, each lasting 45 minutes, in two beautiful coral reef spots.
  • Guided by experts, you'll explore vibrant marine life in a safe and exciting way.
  • After snorkeling, enjoy a freshly prepared lunch onboard while soft music sets a relaxing mood.
  • Then comes the highlight: a 2-hour stay on Giftun Island, where you can swim, sunbathe, relax,
  • or take stunning photos in this natural paradise—perfect for families, couples, and groups.
  • After your island time, the boat returns you to the marina and your hotel,
  • leaving you with unforgettable memories of this private Red Sea escape.
