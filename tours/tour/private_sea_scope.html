Description
  • Embark on an unforgettable Red Sea adventure with Hurghada's famous Sea Scope submarine boat tour.
  • Your journey begins with complimentary hotel transfers from anywhere in Hurghada to the marina, where the state-of-the-art Sea Scope vessel awaits.
  • The submarine features a unique panoramic viewing chamber with large glass windows, offering crystal-clear views of the magical underwater world.
  • After 15 minutes of sailing, you’ll descend to the lower chamber to observe the rich marine ecosystem.
  • Enjoy a 30-minute snorkeling session at pristine coral reefs — equipment provided.
  • Return to the submarine to continue observing colorful coral formations and rare tropical fish through panoramic windows.
  • Expert marine guides provide detailed explanations about fish species and ecosystems while you enjoy cold refreshments.
  • This experience is perfect for families, seniors, and anyone seeking a safe, air-conditioned environment to explore the Red Sea.
  • Return to the marina filled with unforgettable memories of the enchanting underwater world.
