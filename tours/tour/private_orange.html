Destinations
  • Pick up from hotel and transfer to Hurghada Marina.

  • Upon arrival, you have full freedom to choose how to enjoy your time:
    • Relax on the pristine white sandy beach.
    • Swim in the calm turquoise waters.

  • Transfer back to your hotel with unforgettable memories.
Description
  • Book the best Orange Bay Island trip in Hurghada — a luxurious 4-hour tropical journey to a hidden paradise in the Red Sea.
  • Your day begins with a comfortable hotel transfer to the marina, where your private speedboat — equipped with all luxury amenities — awaits.
  • Enjoy complete freedom as you sail across turquoise waters to Orange Bay Island, a secluded tropical escape perfect for families and friends.
  • Upon arrival, choose your own adventure: lounge on silky white sands, swim in warm shallow waters ideal for children, or explore vibrant coral reefs with provided snorkeling gear.
  • There’s no rigid schedule — just pure relaxation and privacy in a peaceful tropical atmosphere.
  • Cold drinks and fresh tropical fruits are served throughout the tour to keep you refreshed under the golden sun.
  • Want to explore more? Customize your trip and visit other islands like Sand Island or Hula Hula Island (additional cost based on distance).
  • After 4 hours of unforgettable island time, return to the marina and enjoy a smooth drop-off at your hotel — filled with memories of paradise.
