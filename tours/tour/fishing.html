Description
  • Embark on an authentic deep sea fishing adventure in the pristine crystal-clear waters of the Red Sea with our specialized 4-hour fishing expedition.
  • Your journey begins with convenient pickup from your hotel in Hurghada, El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, or Makadi (small surcharge applies for Safaga), ensuring comfortable transportation to our marina.
  • Aboard our professionally equipped fishing vessel with state-of-the-art fishing gear, you'll head to the Red Sea's premier fishing spots.
  • Our expert multilingual fishing guide, with years of local experience, knows the sea's secrets and employs the best techniques for catching each fish species.
  • During the dedicated 4-hour fishing session, your experience focuses entirely on pure fishing without any other activities, providing you with an authentic and productive fishing adventure.
  • You'll have opportunities to catch diverse and exciting Red Sea fish species using various fishing methods including bottom fishing and surface fishing techniques.
  • Throughout your fishing expedition, enjoy premium hospitality including cold refreshing beverages (bottled water,soft drinks), fresh seasonal fruits to keep you energized.
  • The Red Sea around Hurghada offers exceptional fishing opportunities with over 35 different fish species, from parrotfish and sea bream to challenging big game fish like barracuda and yellowfin tuna.
  • At the end of your fishing adventure, return to the marina with beautiful memories and potentially some fresh fish you caught with your own hands, before we safely transport you back to your hotel.
