description
  • Experience the vibrant beauty of the Red Sea with our Hula Hula Island group day trip,
  • expertly designed for visitors who seek a straightforward and enjoyable marine adventure.
  • Pickup begins between 8:00–9:00 AM from hotels across Hurghada,
  • Makadi Bay, Sahl Hasheesh, and El Gouna (Safaga may incur a small extra fee).
  • From the marina, your shared boat cruise sets off across calm turquoise waters to reach Hula Hula Island.
  • Two guided snorkeling sessions await—each around 45 minutes—where professional equipment and safety guidance allow you to explore colorful reefs populated by tropical fish
  • A short boating interval (~30 minutes) separates the two sites, letting you catch your breath before diving again.
  • Upon reaching the island, enjoy up to two hours on soft white sands—perfect for swimming,
    photography, or simply taking in the serene marine atmosphere.
  • Before landing, a delicious lunch with refreshing soft drinks is served onboard—no frills, just satisfying, genuine food designed to refuel you for the rest of the day
  • After lunch, add some fun with included water sports,
  • such as Banana Boat or Sofa Boat rides—ideal for laughter and shared group energy
  • The boat returns to shore by late afternoon, followed by hotel drop-off adding comfort to your seamlessly organized day. Expect around eight hours total of relaxation,
  • beach beauty, and ocean discovery—no hidden charges or surprises, just a clean, well‑managed tourism experience.
Included
  • Round-trip hotel pickup and drop-off from Hurghada, Makadi Bay, Sahl Hasheesh, El Gouna (no extra cost).
  • Boat cruise and two guided snorkeling stops with full gear.
  • Entry fees for island and marine park included.
  • Up to 2 hours on the island beach.
  • Lunch and soft drinks served onboard.
  • Water activities (Banana Boat, Sofa Boat) included.
  • Experienced snorkeling and tour guides onboard.
Not Included
  • Transfers from Safaga—extra fee applies.
  • Personal activities such as photos, souvenirs, or tips.
  • Bringing food or snacks from outside (strictly not allowed).
What to Bring
  • Swimwear, towel, reef-safe sunscreen, sunglasses, hat.
  • Waterproof pouch or bag for valuables.
  • Waterproof camera or GoPro
What Not to Bring
  • Outside food or drinks (lunch is provided).
  • Large luggage or backpacks—space is limited.
  • Alcoholic beverages or pets.
Pro Tips
  • Be ready between 08:00 and 09:00 AM to ensure timely departure.
  • Follow guide instructions during snorkeling for a safe, quality experience.
  • Stay hydrated before and after snorkeling and water games.
  • Use reef-safe sunscreen to support marine conservation.
  • Secure your belongings with water‑sealed bags during activities
