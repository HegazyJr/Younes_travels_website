Destinations
  • Hotel pickup between 10:00 AM and 10:30 AM (Hurghada, El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, Makadi Bay).
  • Transfer to the adventure station for safety briefing and vehicle assignment.
  • Begin 1-hour ride through desert dunes and valleys to the Bedouin village.
  • Arrive and spend 2 hours exploring the village: meet locals, learn traditions, enjoy tea.
  • Ride a camel for 10 minutes around the village perimeter.
  • Optional: Book a horse ride or deeper camel trek (additional cost).
  • Return 1-hour ride to the station on your chosen vehicle.
  • Final transfer back to your hotel (arrive around 3:30–4:00 PM).
Description
  • Escape the resort and dive into the heart of Egypt’s Eastern Desert with this 5-hour cultural adventure, just 25 km west of Hurghada.
  • Your journey begins with a comfortable pickup from your hotel between 10:00 AM and 10:30 AM.
  • Arrive at the adventure station and choose your ride: a powerful Quad Bike (for solo or tandem riders) or a shared Jeep.
  • Begin your 1-hour ride across golden sand dunes, rocky valleys, and breathtaking desert landscapes to a real Bedouin village.
  • Spend 2 full hours experiencing authentic desert life: sit with locals, learn about traditions, and enjoy sweet Bedouin tea with fresh dates.
  • Take a 10-minute camel ride around the village — a perfect photo opportunity and cultural highlight.
  • For more adventure, upgrade to a horse ride or an extended camel tour into deeper desert trails (extra cost).
  • After your cultural immersion, return on your chosen vehicle for another thrilling 1-hour ride back to the station.
  • Enjoy a final transfer in an air-conditioned vehicle back to your hotel, arriving by mid-afternoon with unforgettable memories of real Egyptian desert life.
  • This is a daytime adventure focused on culture, nature, and authentic interaction — no dinner or shows, just pure desert authenticity.
Included
  • Round-trip hotel transfer (Hurghada, El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, Makadi Bay)
  • 1-hour Jeep or Quad Bike ride to the village (and 1-hour return)

  • 10-minute camel ride with traditional tea and dates
  • Professional guide and safety equipment
  • Comprehensive travel insurance
Not Included
  • Horse riding or extended camel tour (available with extra cost)
  • Professional photography in traditional Bedouin costume
  • Extra food or drinks beyond included tea
  • Souvenirs or personal purchases
  • Tips (gratuities) for guides or villagers
What to Bring
  • Comfortable clothing suitable for desert (long sleeves recommended)
  • Sturdy closed-toe shoes or sports shoes
  • Sunglasses, scarf, or hat to protect from sun and sand
  • Camera or phone (with extra battery or power bank)
  • Cash for optional activities or small village purchases
What Not to Bring
  • Outside food or drinks (tea is provided)
  • Large bags or heavy luggage (limited vehicle space)
  • Alcoholic beverages or pets
  • White or light-colored clothing (will get dusty)
Pro Tips
  • Be ready by 10:00 AM for timely pickup.
  • Choose your ride (Jeep or Quad Bike) when booking.
  • Wear layers — it’s hot during the day, but shade is limited.
  • Drink plenty of water before and during the tour.
  • Apply and reapply sunscreen every 2 hours.
  • Keep electronics in sealed bags to avoid sand damage.
  • Respect local customs — ask before photographing people.
  • Start and end times may vary slightly depending on season and hotel location.
