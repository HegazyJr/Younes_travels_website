description
  • Embark on the greatest desert adventure ever - the Complete Adventure Tour that combines all types of desert vehicles in one action-packed day filled with excitement and Bedouin culture from 11:00 AM to 7:00 PM.
  • This tour is designed for ultimate adventure seekers and comprehensive desert experience enthusiasts.
  • Your exceptional journey begins with hotel pickup from Hurghada, El Gouna, Sahel Hashish, or Makadi Bay at 11:00 AM - with Safaga pickup available for additional fees.
  • From the safari station, you'll enjoy an epic 4×4 jeep ride covering 80 kilometers through desert mountains, deep valleys, and rugged trails to the deepest points of the Eastern Desert.
  • During the mountain journey, you'll enjoy cold beverages and natural water to maintain your energy throughout the long day.
  • You'll have a deep cultural experience in the remote Bedouin village where you explore authentic traditional life and learn traditional Bedouin handicrafts from local residents.
  • Enjoy a complete traditional Bedouin lunch, extended camel riding (additional fees apply), and authentic tea with folk heritage stories.
  • After lunch comes the most powerful adventures - one and a half hours of quad biking across all terrain types from massive sand dunes to rocky valleys.
  • Followed by the thrilling buggy experience (dune buggy) for half an hour of pure adrenaline and crazy maneuvers.
  • In the evening, you'll move to the highest sand dune for spectacular sunset viewing, then enjoy a luxury barbecue dinner in the desert under the stars with the complete oriental show.
  • For more excitement, additional night tours with all vehicles are available for extra fees - perfect for extreme adventure lovers.
  • You'll return to your hotel in the evening filled with unforgettable adventure memories and comprehensive desert experience.

  • Hotel transfers to and from (Hurghada, El Gouna, Sahel Hashish, Makadi Bay)

What to Bring
  • Comfortable, layered clothing to adapt to temperature changes
  • Strong, comfortable sports shoes for all activities and different vehicles
  • Camera with large memory and extra battery for the long journey documentation
  • Medium-sized, tightly sealed bag to protect items from sand
  • Sufficient cash for purchases, photography, and additional activities
What Not to Bring
  • Outside food (two complete meals included)
  • Large bags or heavy luggage for the long journey
  • Alcoholic beverages (long trip requires full concentration)
  • Unnecessary valuables to avoid damage or loss
Pro Tips
  • Be prepared for a full day of adventures (11:00 AM - 7:00 PM)
  • Have a nutritious, light breakfast before the long journey
  • Listen carefully to safety instructions for each vehicle - safety first
  • Enjoy every moment and take advantage of all available activities
  • Maintain energy levels by drinking fluids regularly throughout the day
  • Interact with local Bedouins and listen to their stories and rich heritage
  • Take appropriate breaks between different activities to avoid fatigue
  • Enjoy the diversity of landscapes - from mountains to dunes to villages
