Description
  • Embark on a spiritual and breathtaking experience — watch the sunrise from the highest peaks of Egypt’s Eastern Desert, one of the most beautiful natural experiences in Hurghada.
  • This tour is specially designed for nature lovers and morning adventure enthusiasts, running from early dawn to mid-morning.
  • Your journey begins with hotel pickup from Hurghada, El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, or Makadi Bay in the refreshing dawn atmosphere (Safaga pickup available at extra cost).
  • From the safari station, enjoy an exciting 4x4 jeep ride through desert valleys and mountain highlands toward the prime sunrise viewing point.
  • Warm beverages and natural water are provided to energize your morning.
  • Experience a unique spiritual moment as the golden sun rises over the endless desert, painting the sky in breathtaking colors.
  • After sunrise, enjoy an adrenaline-filled quad biking session across golden sand dunes.
  • Visit an authentic Bedouin village for rest, camel riding, tea, dates, and local delicacies.
  • Conclude with a healthy light Bedouin breakfast in the serene morning ambiance.
  • Optional upgrades: horse riding or extended camel safari (extra cost). Return to your hotel refreshed, filled with positive energy for the rest of your day.

What to Bring
  • Warm clothes for cool dawn, light clothes for sunrise
  • Closed sports shoes suitable for sand and rocks
  • Camera or phone with sufficient storage

What Not to Bring
  • Heavy food (breakfast included)

Pro Tips
  • Wake up early — ensure good sleep the night before.
  • Have a light snack before departure; avoid overeating.
  • Bring a warm jacket — remove layers as the sun rises.
  • Enjoy the dawn silence — a rare meditative experience.
  • Keep your camera ready — sunrise moment is fleeting!
  • Drink fluids moderately — long journey ahead.
  • Ask guides for best photo angles and viewing spots.
  • Take time to contemplate — this is a rare chance for peace.
