Description
  • Begin your day with a relaxing and private experience aboard a yacht exclusively for your family or friends.
  • Pickup is arranged from Hurghada or surrounding areas (with a small fee from Safaga), taking you to the marina where your yacht awaits.
  • The trip includes two snorkeling stops in vibrant coral reef sites, full of colorful marine life.
  • Guided by professionals, each session ensures a safe, enjoyable, and well-organized experience.
  • Between snorkeling stops, enjoy a hot lunch served with cold and hot drinks while cruising the turquoise waters.
  • Upon arriving at Paradise Island, you'll have about two hours to swim, sunbathe, or unwind on the quiet beach.
  • The tour concludes with a smooth return to the marina and transfer back to your hotel.
  • This trip is ideal for those seeking a peaceful, private, and professionally managed Red Sea adventure.
