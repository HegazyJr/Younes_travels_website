Flexible Royal Valley Options

Option 1:
Valley of the Kings Only
Visit 3–4 pharaohs’ tombs with elaborate decorations. More time for photography and detailed exploration of male royal burial practices.

Option 2:
Valley of the Queens Only
Explore tombs of queens and princes with exceptional art preservation. Quieter, more intimate access with vibrant colors.

Option 3:
Both Valleys (Most Comprehensive)
Visit both Valleys (2–3 tombs each). Compare male vs. female tomb designs, art, and symbolism.

Note: Valley of Kings entrance is €20 more per person than Valley of Queens. Fees paid separately.

  • Return to Hurghada at exactly 9:00 PM after a full day of wonder, discovery, and ancient history.

What to Bring
  • Valid passport or ID
  • Light, modest cotton clothing (no shorts or sleeveless)
  • Camera with extra batteries and memory

  • Cash (EGP, USD, EUR for fees)

Pro Tips
  • Be ready by 4:45 AM for 5:00 AM departure.
  • Discuss preferences with your guide to optimize your day.
  • Carry 4–5 liters of water — no water provided.
  • Wear light-colored cotton to avoid heat stroke.
  • Reapply sunscreen every 2 hours.
  • Ask your guide about tomb differences to make informed choices.
  • Return is at 9:00 PM sharp — plan hotel rest accordingly.

Safety Advisory

CRITICAL: Bring 4–5 liters of water per person. No water is provided except one bottle at lunch.

Health Risks in Heat: Dizziness, nausea, dry mouth, rapid heartbeat — inform guide immediately.

Prevention: Drink water regularly, wear light clothing, apply sunscreen, avoid caffeine.
