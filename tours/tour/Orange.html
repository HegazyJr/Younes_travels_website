Description
  • Begin your day with a comfortable air-conditioned pickup from your hotel in Hurghada, Makadi Bay, Sahl Hasheesh, or El Gouna.
  • Board a well-maintained boat and cruise across pristine Red Sea waters toward Orange Bay Island, nestled within the Giftun Marine Reserve—a protected corridor renowned for its clean environment and thriving marine life.
  • Your adventure starts with the first guided snorkeling session (~45 minutes) amid healthy coral reefs and colorful fish.
  • After a short boat cruise (~30 minutes), you dive again at the second reef site—offering a fresh underwater perspective.
  • Once you reach the island, enjoy up to two hours of leisure time on its soft, golden-orange sand—ideal for swimming, sunbathing, or scenic photography in a serene and protected setting.
  • Before landing, savor a tasty midday lunch with cold drinks on board—a satisfying and fuss-free meal that energizes you for island time.
  • Following lunch, the fun continues with inclusive water sports like Banana Boat and Sofa Boat rides—creating memorable group moments.
  • As the day progresses, the boat returns to the marina and you receive a relaxed hotel drop-off.
  • This seven to eight hour tour seamlessly blends nature, relaxation, and adventure—transparent service, no hidden fees, and pure Giftun beauty in one organized experience.
