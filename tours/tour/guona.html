Description
  • El Gouna is an exclusive resort town located on Egypt's Red Sea coast near Hurghada,
  • originally developed by Orascom Hotels and Development in 1990.
  • The resort is situated 22km north of Hurghada International Airport and 470km from Cairo.
  • Your adventure begins with a comfortable 30-minute air-conditioned coach journey from your Hurghada hotel to this extraordinary resort destination.
  • El Gouna transformed from desert wilderness into an unparalleled resort town, where its founder discovered the most beautiful spot along the Red Sea coastline.
  • Abu Tig Marina: The beating heart of El Gouna, featuring international restaurants, elegant cafes, and upscale boutiques.
  • Enjoy waterfront promenades and capture stunning photos with luxury yachts as your backdrop.
  • El Gouna Downtown: The bustling commercial center offering local markets, authentic restaurants, and traditional coffeehouses.
  • Experience authentic local culture while shopping for unique souvenirs and handicrafts.
  • Lagoons and Waterways: El Gouna's distinctive network of lagoons and colorful bridges connecting different areas of the town
  • earned it the nickname "Venice of the Middle East" - a truly magical experience.
  • Water Activities: Enjoy optional thrilling speedboat excursions through the scenic lagoons for a unique perspective of the resort,
  • plus opportunities to book boat trips to nearby islands and coral reefs.
  • Duty-Free Shopping: Take advantage of excellent duty-free shopping opportunities in Hurghada, featuring international perfumes, jewelry, accessories, and luxury souvenirs at tax-free prices.
  • Pristine Beaches: El Gouna boasts several private resort beaches with day-pass access available, where you can enjoy crystal-clear turquoise waters and soft sandy shores.
  • Your memorable day concludes with an evening return to Hurghada, carrying unforgettable memories of this unique Red Sea resort paradise.

Pro Tips
  • Start early to maximize your full-day experience
  • Bring cash for shopping and beverage purchases
  • Abu Tig Marina restaurants are pricier than Downtown options

  • Keep emergency contacts and hotel information with you
  • Try authentic Egyptian coffee and tea at traditional cafes
  • Capture plenty of photos at the colorful bridges and waterways
