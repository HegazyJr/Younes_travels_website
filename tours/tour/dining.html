Description
  • Embark on an enchanting romantic journey watching sunset from the most beautiful locations in Egypt's Eastern Desert — perfect experience for couples and friends extending from afternoon until evening.
  • This tour is carefully designed to combine excitement, romance, and Bedouin culture in stunning evening atmosphere.
  • Your evening journey begins with hotel pickup from Hurghada, El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, or Makadi Bay in perfect afternoon ambiance (Safaga pickup available with additional cost).
  • From our safari station, enjoy complete quad biking adventure across golden sand dunes and desert valleys toward the traditional Bedouin village.
  • Throughout your journey, refreshing cold drinks and natural water keep you energized and revitalized.
  • Experience authentic cultural immersion in the Bedouin village where you explore traditional lifestyle and interact with local residents.
  • Enjoy camel riding around the village and savor authentic Bedouin tea with dates and local sweets in peaceful, comfortable evening atmosphere.
  • The tour's most memorable moments come when you take your quad to the highest sand dunes for spectacular sunset viewing — a breathtaking natural spectacle providing perfect backdrop for romantic memorable photos.
  • After sunset, indulge in luxurious BBQ dinner in the heart of the desert under starlit sky with gentle Bedouin music.
  • Optional experiences: horse riding adventure or extended camel safari (additional cost).
  • At tour's conclusion, watch a short folklore show reflecting authentic Bedouin heritage, or simply relax and enjoy absolute evening desert tranquility.
  • For extra excitement, additional quad tours under starlight are available at modest cost — perfect for couples seeking romantic adventure.
  • Return to your hotel in the evening filled with unforgettable romantic memories and stunning desert sunset photographs.

What to Bring
  • Comfortable clothes with light jacket for slightly cool evening
  • Closed sports shoes suitable for sand and dunes
  • Camera with sunset settings or phone with good night lighting
  • Small bag for personal items with sand protection
  • Cash for optional purchases and Bedouin gifts
What Not to Bring
  • Outside food (BBQ dinner included and luxurious)
  • Large bags or heavy luggage during quad riding
  • Alcoholic beverages (not permitted on desert tours)
  • Very light-colored or impractical desert clothing
Pro Tips
  • Be ready on time for departure (exactly 2:00 PM)
  • Bring camera ready for most beautiful sunset moments — perfect natural lighting
  • Enjoy evening tranquility and relax during sunset viewing — unique meditative moment
  • Drink sufficient water before and during tour to avoid dehydration
  • Keep light jacket as desert cools slightly with sunset
  • Interact with Bedouins and enjoy their heritage stories around dinner fire
  • Use sunscreen even at sunset time as sun remains strong
  • Enjoy silence and tranquility during sunset — exceptional spiritual experience
