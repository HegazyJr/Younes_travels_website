Description
  • Embark on an amazing exploratory journey that takes you through the history and present of Hurghada city — from the ancient heritage neighborhoods to the modern marina overlooking the Red Sea.
  • This tour is specially designed for culture, heritage, and shopping enthusiasts and is available at any time of day from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM to suit your schedule.
  • Your cultural journey begins with departure from your hotel in a comfortable air-conditioned vehicle with a specialized tour guide fluent in German, Russian, and English, expert in Hurghada's rich history.
  • Your first stop will be in the famous heritage district "El Dahar" — the beating heart of Hurghada pulsing with authentic popular life.
  • You'll stroll through the traditional popular market filled with fragrant spices, authentic Egyptian seasonings, and traditional handmade products.
  • You'll visit the oldest Coptic church in the city, dating back over a century, where you'll learn about the history of Christianity in the region and traditional religious rituals.
  • Afterward, you'll move to visit the Grand Marina Mosque — a modern architectural masterpiece reflecting contemporary Islamic character with enchanting sea views.
  • You'll explore the traditional fishing port and popular fish market, watching fishermen sell their fresh daily catch of famous Red Sea fish.
  • At modern Hurghada Marina, you'll stroll along the upscale promenade among luxury yachts and elegant cafes.
  • Next, we head to Sheraton Street, where you can explore vibrant tourist bazaars and enjoy picking up unique souvenirs to remember your trip.
  • Conclude with free shopping time in modern centers and duty-free stores.
  • An optional traditional Egyptian lunch can be arranged for a complete authentic experience.
    • "The itinerary order may change."
