Description
  • Embark on a day filled with ocean bliss and island calm with our Paradise Island group trip.
  • Your journey starts with pickup from your hotel in Hurghada, El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, or Makadi — Safaga pickup requires a small surcharge.
  • From the marina, sail across the crystal-clear Red Sea toward the serene shores of Paradise Island.
  • You’ll enjoy two guided snorkeling sessions in flourishing coral reef areas brimming with marine life.
  • Each session is carefully timed to allow you to relish the underwater world — from tropical fish to living coral formations.
  • After the first snorkeling, you’ll return briefly to the boat to refresh before your second aquatic adventure in another stunning location.
  • Midday greets you with a satisfying hot lunch and refreshing hot and cold beverages served onboard while cruising.
  • Upon arrival at Paradise Island, you’ll have nearly two hours to wander the soft white sands, swim in calm shallow waters, or simply relax and soak in the scenic surroundings.
  • For added excitement, optional fun rides like banana boat or sofa boat await — great for laughter and group bonding.
  • You’ll return to the marina and be comfortably driven back to your hotel in the late afternoon, ending your trip full of rejuvenation and peaceful memories.

What to Bring
  • Swimwear, towel, reef-safe sunscreen, sunglasses, hat
  • Waterproof camera or GoPro

What Not to Bring

Pro Tips
  • Be ready by 8:00–9:00 AM for seamless departure.
  • Listen to the guide for a safer and smoother snorkeling experience.
  • Stay hydrated before and after water activities.
  • Use reef-safe sunscreen to protect marine ecosystems.
  • Secure valuables in waterproof pouches during excursions.
