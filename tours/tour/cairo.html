Destinations
  • Hurghada > Eastern Desert > Cairo > Giza Pyramids > Egyptian Museum
description
  • Begin your exciting journey to Cairo, Egypt's historic capital, with an early departure from your Hurghada hotel in a luxury air-conditioned bus.
  • The journey takes approximately five hours through the Eastern Desert with a rest stop for refreshments.
  • Upon arrival in Cairo, you'll head directly to the Giza Plateau to visit the three Great Pyramids - Khufu, Khafre, and Menkaure - plus the legendary Sphinx statue.
  • The group will be divided with specialized guides to explain Pharaonic history and ancient construction secrets.
  • After the Pyramids tour, you'll enjoy a traditional Egyptian lunch at one of the certified local restaurants.
  • Then proceed to visit the Egyptian Museum in Tahrir to discover Pharaonic civilization treasures, including Tutankhamun's golden collection and royal sarcophagi.
  • Throughout the day, you'll have sufficient time for photography and exploration at each site, with detailed guidance about Egypt's ancient history and civilization.
  • Return to Hurghada in the evening with expected arrival in late evening.
