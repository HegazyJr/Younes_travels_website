description
  • Enjoy a private tour designed especially for you and your family or group to Cairo.
  • Your journey begins at exactly 4:00 AM from your hotel, with a private modern air-conditioned vehicle with professional driver and specialized tour guide accompanying you all day.
  • The journey takes approximately 4.5-5 hours with possibility to stop for rest and refreshments.
  • Upon arrival in Cairo, you can choose which sites you want to visit from the basic list or add new sites according to available time.
  • The basic program includes: Giza Pyramids and Sphinx, and Egyptian Museum.
  • You can add sites such as: Citadel and historic mosques, Saqqara and Memphis, Khan el-Khalili, or Downtown.
  • You can also remove any site if you prefer to spend more time in a specific place.
  • The specialized tour guide will be with you from departure until return, explaining Egypt's ancient history and civilization at each site you visit.
  • You can spend the time you want at each site and choose your preferred restaurant for lunch.
  • Return to Hurghada is exactly at 10:00 PM, ensuring you have a complete and organized day in Cairo.
