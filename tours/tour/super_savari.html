Description
  • Embark on an unforgettable adventure through Egypt's Eastern Desert — one of the world’s most spectacular landscapes, located just 25 km west of Hurghada.
  • This carefully crafted experience combines thrilling quad biking with authentic Bedouin culture from 2:00 PM to 8:30 PM.
  • Your journey begins with convenient hotel pickup from Hurghada, El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, or Makadi Bay (Safaga pickup available with small fee).
  • From our safari station, enjoy 1.5 hours of exhilarating quad biking across golden dunes and desert valleys.
  • Complimentary refreshing drinks and fresh fruits keep you energized throughout.
  • Visit an authentic Bedouin village to explore ancient desert lifestyle and traditional crafts.
  • Enjoy camel riding and savor authentic Bedouin tea with dates and sweets in magical surroundings.
  • As evening falls, indulge in a luxurious BBQ dinner under breathtaking starlit skies.
  • Watch our complete Oriental show: belly dance, Tanoura, and fire performance.
  • End the night with star gazing and shisha in the majestic desert silence — or opt for an extra quad ride (additional cost).
  • Return to your hotel at 8:30 PM with unforgettable memories of culture, adventure, and desert magic.

What to Bring
  • Comfortable desert-appropriate clothing
  • Closed sports shoes (sand protection)
  • Sunglasses, scarf or hat for sand
  • Camera or phone with full battery & memory
  • Small bag for essentials
  • Cash for souvenirs and photos
What Not to Bring
  • Outside food or drinks (dinner fully included)
  • Large bags or heavy luggage
  • Alcoholic beverages or pets
  • White or light-colored clothing (will get sandy)
Pro Tips
  • Be ready on time — departure is exactly at 2:00 PM.
  • Listen carefully to guide instructions for safe quad operation.
  • Drink plenty of water before and during the tour.
  • Apply and reapply sunscreen every 2 hours.
  • Keep valuables in a sand-proof sealed bag.
  • Interact with Bedouins — learn their culture and stories.
  • Stay on designated paths — don’t separate from the group.
  • Enjoy the desert silence and star gazing — it’s unforgettable.
