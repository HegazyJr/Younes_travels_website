Highlight
  • Flexible experience – stay anywhere from 3 to 8 hours based on your preference.
  • Home to Hurghada’s most beautiful coral reef, located just meters from the shore.
  • Golden sandy beach with shallow, calm waters – perfect for families with kids.
  • Luxurious open buffet lunch with a wide variety of local and international dishes.
  • World-renowned diving and snorkeling destination with vibrant marine life.
  • Multiple water activities: jet ski, banana boat, parasailing, windsurfing, kayaking.
  • Beach fun: horse riding, camel riding, volleyball, darts, petanque.
  • 40km south of Hurghada with comfortable air-conditioned transport.
  • Professional multilingual guides for safety and assistance.
Destinations
  • Hurghada > Desert Road > Sharm El Naga Bay
Description
  • Embark on an exceptional journey to Sharm El Naga, one of the Red Sea's most stunning coastal destinations.
  • This peaceful bay lies 40 km from Hurghada and is renowned for its beautiful coral reefs and crystal-clear waters.
  • Your adventure begins with a comfortable one-hour ride in an air-conditioned coach through breathtaking desert landscapes.
  • Upon arrival, you'll be amazed by the golden sandy beach and protected house reef — perfect for snorkeling and diving, located just meters from the shoreline.
  • It’s ideal for families with children or those who prefer shallow water.
  • Explore vibrant coral reefs, diverse fish species, and maybe even encounter sea turtles or rays — one of the world’s best snorkeling spots.
  • Indulge in a sumptuous open buffet lunch featuring local and international cuisine.
  • Adventure seekers can enjoy jet skiing, parasailing, banana boat rides, windsurfing, horse riding, camel riding, and beach volleyball.
  • Whether you relax on the beach, dive into the underwater world, or try thrilling activities, you’ll return with unforgettable memories.
  • The combination of pristine nature, excellent facilities, and flexible timing makes Sharm El Naga the perfect escape from busy resort life.
Included
  • Round-trip air-conditioned transport from your hotel
  • Sharm El Naga entrance fees
  • Luxurious open buffet lunch (local & international dishes)
  • Free snorkeling equipment (masks, fins, life jackets, towels)
  • Professional snorkeling guide
  • Access to basic facilities (restrooms, changing rooms)
  • Beach chairs and umbrellas
  • Multilingual tour guides
  • Travel insurance
Not Included
  • Paid water activities (jet ski, parasailing, banana boat, etc.)
  • Horse riding and camel riding
  • Additional beverages (beyond basic water/soft drinks)
  • Professional photography services
  • Personal purchases or souvenirs
  • Gratuities (tips)
What to Bring
  • Swimwear and extra clothing
  • Large beach towel
  • High-protection sunscreen (SPF 50+)
  • Sunglasses and wide-brimmed hat
  • Waterproof camera or GoPro
  • Water shoes or rubber sandals for reef protection
  • Cash for optional activities
What Not to Bring
  • Outside food (buffet lunch is provided)
  • Valuable or large bags
  • Expensive jewelry or watches
  • Alcoholic beverages
Pro Tips
  • Start early (7:00–8:00 AM) to make the most of your day.
  • Bring enough cash for optional activities.
  • Use waterproof sunscreen and reapply regularly.
  • Drink plenty of water in the hot weather.
  • Respect the coral reefs — never touch or stand on them.
  • Follow your snorkeling guide’s instructions for safety.
  • Keep valuables in a secure locker.
  • Enjoy the flexibility — leave after 3 hours or stay for 8.
  • Try local dishes at the buffet — they’re delicious!
