Destinations
  • Pickup from your hotel between 6:00 AM and 6:30 AM (Hurghada, El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, Makadi Bay).
  • Transfer to the Quad Bike station for safety briefing and vehicle assignment.
  • Begin 1-hour Quad Bike ride through golden dunes and desert valleys to the Bedouin village.
  • Arrive at the village around sunrise and spend ~1 hour exploring, meeting locals, and enjoying tea.
  • Return on your Quad Bike for another scenic 1-hour ride back to the station.
  • Final transfer back to your hotel — arrive by 11:00 AM, refreshed and inspired.
Description
  • Wake up to magic with this peaceful yet thrilling morning adventure in Egypt’s Eastern Desert, just 25 km west of Hurghada.
  • Your journey begins with an early pickup between 6:00 AM and 6:30 AM from your hotel.
  • Arrive at the Quad Bike station, where you’ll receive a safety briefing and be assigned your own powerful machine.
  • At sunrise, begin your 1-hour ride across golden sand dunes, rocky valleys, and untouched desert trails — the cool morning air and soft light make this a photographer’s dream.
  • Arrive at an authentic Bedouin village and step into a world of tradition and hospitality.
  • Spend about an hour learning about local life, meeting friendly villagers, and sipping sweet, aromatic Bedouin tea with fresh dates.
  • After this cultural pause, hop back on your Quad Bike for a 1-hour return ride through the awakening desert.
  • Back at the station, enjoy a quick refresh before your comfortable transfer back to the hotel.
  • The tour ends around 11:00 AM — perfect for enjoying the rest of your day at the beach or by the pool.
  • This is not just a ride — it’s a serene, soul-refreshing start to your day in the heart of the desert.
Included
  • Round-trip hotel transfer (Hurghada, El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, Makadi Bay)
  • 2-hour Quad Bike ride (1 hour to village, 1 hour return)
  • Visit to authentic Bedouin village with tea session
  • Professional guide and safety gear
  • Comprehensive travel insurance
Not Included
  • Hotel pickup from Safaga (available with extra fee)
  • Alcoholic or special drinks
  • Professional photography in traditional attire
  • Souvenirs or personal purchases
  • Tips (gratuities) for guide or villagers
What to Bring
  • Light, breathable clothing (long sleeves recommended)
  • Sturdy closed-toe shoes
  • Sunglasses, scarf, or hat for sun and sand protection
  • Camera or phone (great for sunrise shots)
  • Small backpack for essentials
What Not to Bring
  • Outside food or drinks
  • Large bags or heavy luggage
  • Alcoholic beverages or pets
  • White or light-colored clothing (will get dusty)
Pro Tips
  • Be ready by 6:00 AM for smooth pickup.
  • Apply sunscreen before departure — sun rises fast!
  • Bring a light jacket — mornings can be cool before sunrise.
  • Stay hydrated — drink water before and after the ride.
  • Keep phones and cameras in sealed bags to avoid sand.
  • Follow the guide’s instructions — safety first.
  • Ride in line with the group; no solo detours.
  • This tour runs daily — ideal for early risers and photographers.
