Destinations

Description
  • Set sail with your family or close friends to Hola Hola Island – a hidden paradise in the Red Sea.
  • Unlike crowded destinations, this exclusive tour offers privacy and tranquility at its finest.
  • A private boat awaits to take you on a scenic journey through sparkling waters, stopping first at two stunning coral reef locations.
  • Here, you'll enjoy guided snorkeling experiences led by professionals – perfect for beginners and seasoned swimmers alike.
  • After exploring the underwater world, relax on the peaceful beaches of Hola Hola Island.
  • The atmosphere is calm, the sand is soft, and the views are truly breathtaking.
  • Back on the boat, savor a freshly prepared lunch, complemented by refreshing soft drinks and local seasonal fruits.
  • This tour is ideal for families or small groups seeking a personalized Red Sea experience.
  • With no loud crowds or rushed schedules, Hola Hola lets you connect with nature and each other.
  • This is more than a trip – it’s a memory in the making.
Included
  • Private boat for your group only
  • Snorkeling equipment (masks, fins, life jackets)
  • Two snorkeling stops with professional guide
  • Island access and relaxation time on the beach
  • Lunch, soft drinks, and seasonal fruits
  • Hotel transfers (from El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh , Makadi & Hurghada)
Not Included
  • Tips (gratuities)
  • Any personal expenses or souvenirs
  • Transfers from Safaga (extra fee applies)
What to Bring
  • Swimwear and towel
  • Sunblock (SPF 50 recommended) and sunglasses
  • Camera or GoPro for underwater and scenic shots
  • Light clothing and hat for sun protection
What Not to Bring
  • Plastic waste (eco-friendly policy – help protect the island)
  • Heavy luggage or large bags (limited space onboard)
Pro Tips
  • Charge your devices before the trip – no power outlets onboard.
  • Inform us in advance of any food allergies or dietary needs.
  • Morning hours offer the clearest water visibility – perfect for snorkeling.
  • Arrive ready by 8:30 AM for smooth pickup.
  • Use reef-safe sunscreen to protect marine life.
