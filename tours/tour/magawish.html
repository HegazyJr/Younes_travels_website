Destinations
  • Pickup from your hotel in Hurghada, El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, or Makadi Bay.
  • Transfer to Hurghada Marina for departure.
  • Begin a scenic 45-minute boat ride to Magawish Island — the largest island in the area.
  • First guided snorkeling session in a protected coral reef area rich in tropical fish and living corals.
  • Arrival at Magawish Island — enjoy soft white sands, calm turquoise waters, and breathtaking scenery.
  • Enjoy refreshing cold drinks before your second snorkeling session at another vibrant reef spot.
  • Midday lunch served on the beach with a view of the endless Red Sea.
  • Free time to relax, swim, snorkel, or explore the island at your own pace (2–5 hours).
  • Optional: Try banana boat or sofa boat rides (extra cost).
  • Return by boat to the marina followed by hotel drop-off.
Description
  • Start your day with a comfortable pickup from your hotel between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM.
  • Arrive at Hurghada Marina and board your comfortable, well-equipped boat for the 45-minute cruise to Magawish Island — the crown jewel of the Red Sea.
  • During the journey, enjoy free cold drinks and soak in the stunning views of the open sea.
  • Begin with your first guided snorkeling session in a protected coral reef area, where you’ll encounter vibrant marine life including parrotfish, angelfish, and colorful corals.
  • After arriving at Magawish Island, enjoy a refreshing break with cold drinks before your second snorkeling session at another pristine reef location.
  • At midday, savor a delicious lunch served right on the beach, featuring fresh salads, grilled chicken or fish, rice, and bread — all with a panoramic sea view.
  • After lunch, spend 2 to 5 hours (your choice) relaxing on the soft white sand, swimming in the calm, shallow waters, or exploring the island’s natural beauty.
  • For extra excitement, optional water sports like banana boat or sofa boat rides are available at an additional cost — perfect for families and groups.
  • Your professional multilingual guide ensures safety, provides snorkeling instructions, and enhances your experience with local knowledge.
  • After a full day of relaxation and adventure, return to the marina and enjoy a comfortable transfer back to your hotel — carrying unforgettable memories of Egypt’s underwater paradise.

What to Bring
  • Swimwear, towel, reef-safe sunscreen
  • Sunglasses, wide-brimmed hat for sun protection
  • Waterproof camera or GoPro for underwater memories
  • Small waterproof bag for essentials
  • Cash for optional activities and upgrades
What Not to Bring
  • Outside food or drinks (prohibited on the island)
  • Large luggage or heavy bags (limited boat space)
  • Alcoholic beverages or pets
Pro Tips
  • Be ready for pickup between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM.
  • Specify your preferred trip duration (3–7 hours) when booking.
  • Listen carefully to the guide’s snorkeling safety instructions.
  • Stay hydrated — drink water before and after snorkeling.
  • Use reef-safe sunscreen to protect marine life.
  • Keep valuables in waterproof bags during water activities.
  • Book lunch in advance if you have dietary preferences.
  • Do not touch or step on coral reefs — preserve the ecosystem.
