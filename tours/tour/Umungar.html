Description
  • Embark on an extraordinary 3-hour adventure to Umunqar Island, one of the Red Sea's best-kept secrets and most pristine destinations.
  • Your journey begins with a comfortable pickup from your hotel in Hurghada, El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, or Makadi Bay, followed by a scenic boat ride across the crystal-clear turquoise waters to this untouched paradise.
  • What makes Umunqar Island truly special is its raw, natural beauty — no umbrellas, no chairs, just pure wilderness and crystal-clear waters.
  • You'll experience the island exactly as nature intended: pristine sandy shores, transparent waters, and complete tranquility away from crowded tourist spots.
  • The beauty of this tour lies in your complete freedom of choice.
  • Feel like snorkeling? Dive into vibrant underwater worlds teeming with colorful fish and coral formations.
  • Want to try fish feeding? Watch tropical fish gather around you in an unforgettable interactive experience.
  • Prefer to simply relax? Find your perfect spot on the natural beach and soak in the peaceful island atmosphere.
  • Throughout your 3-hour island escape, enjoy refreshing beverages and fresh seasonal fruits served by our attentive crew.
  • Our professional multilingual guides speaking English, German, and Russian ensure your safety and comfort while respecting your desire for independence and relaxation.
  • Return to the marina refreshed and rejuvenated, with memories of one of the Red Sea's most authentic and untouched island experiences.
